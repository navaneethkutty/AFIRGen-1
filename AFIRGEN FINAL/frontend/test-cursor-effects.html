Feature Details

Cursor Trail

A smooth trail of fading particles follows your cursor as you move. The trail adapts to the current theme (light/dark mode).

Ripple Effect

When you click anywhere on the page, an expanding ripple effect emanates from the click position. Multiple ripples can be active simultaneously.

Glow Effect

A subtle glow follows your cursor, creating a soft ambient light effect that enhances the visual experience.

Mobile Detection

Cursor effects are automatically disabled on mobile devices and tablets to ensure optimal performance and avoid unnecessary effects on touch devices.
